# How to Customize Prompts for Specific Clients

## Customization framework

### 1) Business context layer
- Business type: lead gen, ecommerce, SaaS, publisher, marketplace.
- Revenue levers: form fills, demos, subscriptions, transactions.
- Sales cycle length and funnel complexity.

### 2) Audience layer
- Segment by awareness stage and search intent.
- Add pain points, objections, and decision criteria.
- Include user sophistication and terminology.

### 3) Market layer
- Geography (local, national, international).
- Competitive density and SERP volatility.
- Regulatory/compliance constraints.

### 4) SEO maturity layer
- Existing content quality and depth.
- Technical debt profile.
- Historical link authority and brand visibility.

## Customization template (copy/paste)

```text
Before generating the deliverable, adapt your recommendations to:
- Business model: [BUSINESS_MODEL]
- Revenue goal: [REVENUE_GOAL]
- Audience segments: [AUDIENCE_SEGMENTS]
- Geography: [GEOGRAPHIC_LOCATION]
- SEO maturity: [SEO_MATURITY_LEVEL]
- Key constraints: [BUDGET_TIMELINE_CONSTRAINTS]
- Brand voice: [BRAND_VOICE]

Prioritize recommendations by impact, effort, and time-to-result.
```
