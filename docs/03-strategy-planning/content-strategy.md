# Content Strategy Prompts

## Pillar/Cluster and Editorial Planning

### Purpose
Define scalable content architecture aligned with search intent and funnel needs.

### Client Value
Builds topical authority while connecting content to conversion journeys.

### Prompt
```text
Create a content strategy for [WEBSITE_URL] in [INDUSTRY].

Include:
- Content pillar and cluster architecture
- Editorial calendar for 6 months
- Content gap and opportunity map
- Optimization prioritization for existing pages
- Internal linking blueprint across clusters
- KPI model (traffic, engagement, assisted conversions)

Provide recommendations by funnel stage and audience segment.
```

### Expected Output Format
Content strategy document + monthly publishing calendar.

### Tips for Best Results
Add SME availability constraints and publishing capacity inputs.
