Reporting Prompts

Monthly SEO Performance Report

Purpose

Summarize SEO impact and next actions for stakeholders.

Client Value

Improves accountability and keeps execution aligned to business outcomes.

Prompt


Generate a monthly SEO report for [WEBSITE_URL].
Include: KPI dashboard, ranking movements, traffic/conversion changes, backlink growth, competitor shifts, wins/losses, and next-month priorities.

Expected Output Format

Executive summary + analyst appendix + action plan.

Ranking Movement Analysis

Prompt


Analyze ranking movements by keyword cluster over [DATE_RANGE].
Highlight major gains/drops, likely causes, and corrective actions.

Traffic and Conversion Analysis

Prompt


Evaluate organic traffic quality for [WEBSITE_URL], including landing-page performance, assisted conversions, intent alignment, and funnel leakage.

Backlink Growth Report

Prompt


Create a backlink growth report for [WEBSITE_URL] showing referring domain trends, authority quality, anchor text mix, and risk flags.

Competitive Positioning Report

Prompt


Produce a competitive positioning report comparing [WEBSITE_URL] vs [COMPETITOR_URLS] by topic visibility, share-of-voice trends, and strategic opportunities.
