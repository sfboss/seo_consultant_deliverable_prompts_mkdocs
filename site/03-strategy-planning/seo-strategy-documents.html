SEO Strategy Document Prompts

Comprehensive SEO Strategy Roadmap

Purpose

Transform research into a strategic plan aligned to business outcomes.

Client Value

Gives stakeholders a clear path from SEO investment to revenue impact.

Prompt


Act as an SEO director. Create a comprehensive SEO strategy for [WEBSITE_URL].

Context:
- Industry: [INDUSTRY]
- Audience: [TARGET_AUDIENCE]
- Geography: [GEOGRAPHIC_LOCATION]
- Business goals: [PRIMARY_GOALS]
- Key competitors: [COMPETITOR_URLS]

Deliver:
1) Strategic objectives tied to business outcomes
2) Priority matrix (quick wins vs long-term initiatives)
3) Resource allocation (roles, capacity, dependencies)
4) 6-month timeline by workstream
5) KPI framework with leading and lagging indicators
6) Risk register and mitigation strategies

Expected Output Format

Executive strategy brief + operational implementation plan.

Example Usage

Use this as the main deck backbone for stakeholder kickoff.

Follow-up Prompts

  • "Convert the roadmap into weekly sprint plans."
  • "Produce executive one-page version."
